About
A small profile

I studied English and later worked in overseas business operations at Ctrip Guangzhou, where most days were filled with hotel systems, supplier communication, customer complaints, and the quiet pressure of keeping things running smoothly.

Over time, I found myself drawn more toward visual storytelling, writing, and emotionally driven creative work. Now I spend much of my time building small digital projects, creating content, and exploring AI-assisted workflows through images, words, and online spaces. This website is less a polished portfolio and more an ongoing record of taste, emotion, work, and the slow process of becoming someone I can recognize more clearly.
