About Me

Nice to meet you

ISFP · Museum wanderer · Fragrance hunter
Racco Ho ✦

Hi, I'm Racco Ho.

I'm a visual storyteller and AI creator. I studied English (International Communication) at university — which is probably why this site bounces between two languages without apology.

When I'm not designing stickers, I'm wandering through museums and galleries, hunting down the next scent, or capturing quiet moments with my camera. My headphones are usually spinning R&B, alternative pop, or whatever Olivia / Sabrina / Ariana just dropped.

I run a couple of content accounts on Xiaohongshu and Instagram. It started as fun, but I genuinely fell in love with visual storytelling — the way a single image or a well-placed sticker can say what words can't.
